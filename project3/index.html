section one

Natura Exploration was founded by Arnaud Luccioni, who loves nature and French Polynesia. Let him take you through Tahiti for amazing canyoning adventures!on Born of parents specialization in climbing and trekking, Arnaud has fallen into outdoor activities since his youngest age. He went to Tahiti in the 90s where he fell in love with the history, culture, fauna and flora of Polynesia. As a hiking guide and canyoning instructor, he began in year 2000 to offer 4x4 excursions and then trekking, canyoning, and aquatic hiking trips from Puna'auia. In 2017, he partnered with Inès Descamps, a Polynesian woman who is delighted to share with you the stories of her family, as the myths and legends of Tahiti.
